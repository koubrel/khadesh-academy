About Khadesh Academy

Khadesh Academy is an online learning platform offered by Khadesh Consulting in collaboration with universities and educational bodies. Our goal is to equip women and Nigerians under 30 with the training and support needed for personal development and exposure to the digital world.

There are paid and free courses, with a specified study period and an expiration date. Certificates are awarded upon completion.
